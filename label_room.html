PRODUCTION DESIGN
FLOOR GRAPHIC INSTALLATION

Objective: Create a complementary floor graphic for a production area as part of the larger Branding the Buildings initiative to improve the look and feel of shared working environments. Because the production area chosen was the Applied Medical Label Room, a colorful CMYK (cyan, magenta, yellow and black) theme was chosen.

Role: Large wall graphics and quotes had been installed during an earlier portion of the project, but a planned floor graphic had still not moved forward from the conception stage. Working only from a low-resolution Photoshop mockup, I partnered with another designer to measure the room, recreate a floor plan, consult with supervisors of the production area, create and preflight a design file, consult with print production team members to ensure output feasibility, and finally install the vinyl strips in the Label Room.
