ABOUT ME
HI, I'M HEATHER

I started out as a small child living in the Sierra Nevada foothills of California. My creative pursuits at that time included drawing horses and making culinary arrangements out of pine cones.

Now I am a graphic designer living in San Clemente, California. I received a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Communication from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo where I concentrated in Web and Digital Media. I find myself most intrigued by projects that require creative application of technical knowledge, especially in the area of web design. When I am not immersed in a creative project you may find me taking dance classes, reading obscure books, or running on the beach.
