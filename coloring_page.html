CORPORATE REBRAND STRATEGY
TEAM COLORING PAGE

Objective: I was commissioned by the Applied Medical Branding Events Planning Committee to create a large color-by-numbers page that would be installed in a communal area and serve as a team building exercise and raise awareness for the “One Team One Heart” campaign.

Role: I created a vector art coloring page using Adobe illustrator that contained corporate messaging and Applied Medical products hidden within the design. I consulted other designers during the creation of the piece as well as the Print Production Team.
